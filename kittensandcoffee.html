About Us:

Founded in 2015 by Sir Snuggles, Kittens and Coffee aims to help you break away from the busy world and simply settle down for a nice cup of coffee and some snuggles with our kitties. We have worked with many non-profit organizations and animal shelters in the past decade, finding homes for all the fur babies you see in our cafe. In the past 9 years, over 500 cats have been adopted through Kittens and Coffee.
